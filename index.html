About us


Here at 5&10 Auto Repair Shop, we are committed to bringing great quality work to your vehicles. When you bring your vehicles to our shop, you can trust that you are getting honest pricing and dependable work. Our mechanic, Richard, has close to 15 years of experience as a mechanic, and is passionate about the work he puts out. Qualified with many years of experience and a Masters of Engineering in Mechanics, you can rest assured that your vehicle is in great hands. We are a Korean-Canadian run business who welcomes everyone, and are very appreciative of the London community for all the support!
